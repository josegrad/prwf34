I'm going to Boston Comic Con next month, and I've never been to it or to another comic convention before. Any advice or tips for a first-time BCC/ComicCon go-er? Thanks! And to answer your question, I'm most looking forward to Worlds' Finest #1.
I’m from Boston so that’s my home town con. It’s been fun to see the show grow year after year. It is now in the Hynes which is the perfect space. I’d recommend that you decide what you want from the show. Commissions? Than make sure you approach the top artist early (before the show even). Do you want to buy back issues? Than make sure to bring a backpack to carry them and small bills. There are a lot of deals at these shows, I’ve seen boxes and boxes of $5 trades. And bring copies of comics for the creators to sign. Almost of all them sign for free and it’s a great excuse to meet your favorite creator, chat about their craft and thank them for their work.
